After he finished, he ventured into Spain, and he could not go until next year--the year 726, after the building of Rome.

But since the Pannonians and Dalmatians had delayed hem, he meant to have gone against Britans, so the Salassians, who were in Italy and Switzerland, the Cantabrians, and Asturians delayed him.  I do not know between whom this controversy took place, as the information may not be reliable.
