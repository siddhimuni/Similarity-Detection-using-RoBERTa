He cannot accept as final the phenomena of the sensible world,
but looks behind that world into another which rules the sensible one. From this tendency of
the human mind, systems of mythology and scientific theories have equally sprung. By the former
the experiences of volition, passion, power, and design, manifested among ourselves, were transplanted,
with the necessary modifications, into an unseen universe from which the sway and potency of
those magnified human qualities were exerted.
