Chapter 1.

A diminutive boy with a dirty countenance met with another alike diminutive boy caparisoned.  Said the first: 'Eech! you are not aware of how much twicet two is?'

'You are a----'(we censor the word he used; suffice it to say, it may mean,'a kind of harp much used by the ancients!;)--'twicet two is four.

Hmm!' stated the second.

The onlooker may not see it, but the author does, that this puny conversation has crucial bearing on William Ravenshoe's fate, the wrongful-rightful, rightful-wrongful, etc., heir.
