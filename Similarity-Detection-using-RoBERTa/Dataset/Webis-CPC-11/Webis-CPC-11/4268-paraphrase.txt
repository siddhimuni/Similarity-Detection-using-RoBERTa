CHAP. 15.

Of gathering and keeping Fruit. 

{SN: Generall Rule.} {SN: Cherries, &c.} Although it be an easy matter, when God shall send it,together and keep fruit,yet they are certain things worthy your regards.You must gather the ripen fruits and not before,else will it either be tough and sore.Generally all fruit are ripen,when they begin to fall.For trees do as all the others bearers do,when their young ones are ripen wain them.The dour her pigeons, the Cony(fur) of rabbits and women their children.Some fruit tree sometimes get a taint in the setting with a frost or euill wind, will cast his fruit untimely(not proper timing),but not before he leave giving them sap or they leave growing.Except from this foresaid rule, Cherries, Damsons and Bullies.
