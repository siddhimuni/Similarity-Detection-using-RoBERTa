
BARRY PAIN, author of "Eliza" and other novels and short stories of adventure, of many well-known
parodies and poems.

SIR GILBERT PARKER, of Canadian birth, poet and author of romantic novels, including "The Judgment
House," and "The Right of Way."

EDEN PHILLPOTTS, realistic novelist, noted for his exact portraits of the English rustic, author
of "Down Dartmoor Way."

SIR ARTHUR WING PINERO, one of the most popular of living dramatists. His plays include "Sweet
Lavender" and "The Second Mrs. Tanqueray."
