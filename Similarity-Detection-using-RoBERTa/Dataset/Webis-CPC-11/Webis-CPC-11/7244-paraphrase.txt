It was a principle sustain by Hooker and Archbishop Laud as well as by Bishop Cosin, and still later by Archbishop Sheldon in 1670, that the habitude of cathedrals or mother churches was measured to be a pattern for that of parochial churches. 

Wherever, therefore, the Clergy form a company acceptable for concord they ought not to communicate less often than every Sunday, and may well do so oftener, even daily; and wherever a company of congregant laity desire a like exemption, they are not debarred from it by this habit.
