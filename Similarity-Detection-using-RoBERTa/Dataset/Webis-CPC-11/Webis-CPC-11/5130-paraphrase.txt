A republic is a free state without any king and among all the republic countries in the world, A considerable and a powerful republic is the States-General of the United Provinces mainly comprising of seven provinces namely, Guelderland, Zealand, Friesland, Utrecht, Groningen, and Overyssel. Hague, a village in United Provinces is considered to be the most beautiful village in the world. Amsterdam, the capital is also a fine city. Other places of interest in Holland include, Dort, Haerlem, Leyden, Delft and Rotterdam. Throughout Holland, one will observe cleanliness all across the streets, which is much better than what we maintain in our houses. Holland has a huge commerce with China, Japan and most of East Indies.
