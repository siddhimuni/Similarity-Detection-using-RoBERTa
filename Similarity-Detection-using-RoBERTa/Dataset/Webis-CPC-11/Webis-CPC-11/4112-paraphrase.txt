Osborne, 27 July 1857.

Dear uncle, - At this time very marriage [28] is happening - that node is connected to link child a wonderful sweet man well worthy - and I'm sure you'll be more moved. May every blessing attend it! I wish I could attend - but I loved being there half makes me feel like I have been there myself. I try to imagine how everything will be me. I could give a greater proof of my love for you all, and my anxiety and give you pleasure dear Charlotte, dear Albert calling than to overcome - for I encouraged and asked to go, although it may I think combien cela court or as completely déroutée am and feel when he is away, or as the number of hours until I return. All children are just as many anything to me when he is away, it seems that the entire life of the house and went home when he is away!

We do everything we can in our own way fêter very quiet this day dear. We are all mourning addition, children are to have half the holidays, Alice is the first dinner in the evening with us, to drink healths Archduke and Archduchess's and I ordered wine for our staff, and grog for our sailors to do the same.

Vicky (who is painting in the alcove next to me) wants to tell you everything and torque Dear young people, and pray tell Charlotte loved everything we did ....

Here we are in anxiety (and I fear that many people in very cruel) suspense for news in India. They should have arrived the day before yesterday.

Thursday, then we have to Prince Napoleon, and on the following Thursday, the emperor and empress, and after her for a night, Queen of the Netherlands, [29] whose work is amazing - and she sees everything and everyone and go everywhere, it is certainly intelligent and kind ....

Now, with love our children dearly, devoted to all your niece,

VICTORIA R.

Pray give my kind regards to all visitors, even those who do not know. I hope my dear husband will tell me everything about everything. Vicky is constantly talking and thinking of Charlotte.
