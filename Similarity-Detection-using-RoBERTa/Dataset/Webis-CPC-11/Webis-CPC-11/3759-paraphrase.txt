He admitted his mistakes and explained the reasons behind it. In his opinion the loss of intellectual prestige gave way to intellectual comradeship. But this is not a serious diversiom.
     Some masters were disturbed by the political discussions as they stand for intellectual infallibilty.  They  accused the boys of being self-consicous. Obviously  this charge is false as the boys are enthusiasts. They were also charged with pacifism.
           The opposition  argued that if the political education is going to be continued the school will be empty in a couple of years. But actually the statistics shows that political education had no effect at all or it led to a slight increase in the commercial well-being of the school. The material for our experiment were the boys and not our collegues. But the success of our experiment irritated them and they labelled the  success as pacifism and priggery.
