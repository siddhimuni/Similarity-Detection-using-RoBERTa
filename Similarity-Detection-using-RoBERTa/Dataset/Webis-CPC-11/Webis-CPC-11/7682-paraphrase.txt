BARRY PAIN.  Wrote many well-known poems and parodies, such as "Eliza" and other adventure stort stories and novels.

SIR GILBERT PARKER wrote "The Judgement House" and "The Right of Way," among other romantic novels and poems.  He was born in Canada.

EDEN PHILLPOTTS is known best for his exact portraits of the English rustic.  He is a realistic novelist and author of "Down Dartmoor Way."

SIR ARTHUR WING PINERO wrote the plays, "Sweet Lavender" and "The Second Mrs. Tanqueray."  He is one of the most popular living dramatists.
